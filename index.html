MEP Modeling, Drafting & Engineering solutions

We provide comprehensive multi-disciplinary engineering and BIM services, specializing in Civil (Architecture & Structure) and MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing). Our mission is to transform complex design concepts into high-precision digital twins that are ready for construction. By combining technical analysis—such as thermal load calculations and structural integrity—with advanced 3D modeling, we deliver integrated solutions that eliminate site conflicts, optimize material usage, and ensure seamless project execution from start to finish.

>2D Drafting & Detailing: Comprehensive shop drawings and schematic layouts using AutoCAD.
>3D BIM Modeling: High-LOD, data-rich MEP models developed in Revit for seamless coordination.
>Structural & Civil Design: Drafting and modeling of structural frameworks and civil layouts to ensure technical accuracy and safety.
>MEP Engineering & Analysis: Expert heating/cooling load calculations (Carrier HAP), and precision duct/pipe sizing for optimized system performance.
>Estimation & Scheduling: Accurate Bill of Quantities (BOQ) and equipment schedules extracted directly from BIM data for precise budgeting.
>Interdisciplinary Coordination: Advanced clash detection to identify and resolve overlaps between MEP, Structural, and Architectural elements before construction begins.
